Detailed Description

Interface to provide the data required to perform the analysis.

If new analyses are added to libwfa which require additional data, please add the respective functions to the interface.

◆ analysis_type

enum libwfa::wf_analysis_data_i::analysis_type

Types of wave function / density analyses to be performed

Enumerator
NO 

Natural orbital analysis.

DENS_MOM 

Multipole moments of the density.

NDO 

Natural difference orbital analysis.

FORM_AD 

Form attachment/detachment densities.

EXCITON_AD 

Exciton analysis on a/d densities.

NTO 

Natural transition orbital analysis.

TDEN_MOM 

Multipole moments of the transition density.

FORM_EH 

Form electron/hole densities.

EXCITON 

Exciton analysis on transition density.

SA_NTO 

State-averaged NTO analysis.

NTO_ENE 

Compute "energies" of hole and particle NTOs.

DYSON 

Dyson orbital analysis.

NTO_SOC 

Compute SOC integrals of hole and particle NTOs of the universal triplet optdm
