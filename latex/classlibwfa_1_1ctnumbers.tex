\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Charge transfer (CT) number analysis. 

Combines the individual components of the CT number analysis by
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item constructing the $\Omega$ matrices in AOs from transition density matrices
\item computing CT number matrices and the total CT number $ \Omega_{\mathrm{total}}$ . 
\end{DoxyItemize}
